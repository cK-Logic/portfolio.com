About Me

Front-End Developer!

Hi there! My name is Richard, and I'm a 24-year-old Cuban native who now lives in North Carolina. I moved to the United States in 2011 and spent a decade living in Florida before moving to North Carolina. I am bilingual and speak both English and Spanish fluently.

When I'm not studying or working, you can often find me at the bowling alley! Although I'm not particularly good at it, I really enjoy the game and the social atmosphere it provides. I always have a great time meeting new people and cheering them on (even if I'm secretly hoping to improve my own score).

I am proud of my Cuban heritage and cherish the traditions and values instilled in me from a young age. I hope to continue sharing my unique perspective with others and learning from the experiences of those around me.

Thanks for taking the time to get to know me a little better!
